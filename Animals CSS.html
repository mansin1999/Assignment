List of 15 Popular Animals on the Earth.

Animals

Lion:lion is a wild animal. It is called “The King of the Jungle” because of its massive size and raw power. Most of the lion population is found in India in the Jungle. The lion is one of the most powerful land animals. They are also known as the jungle King and they roar in such a way that can be heard up to 8 km distance. . Famous Sanctury in the world for lion - Gir National Park from gujarat.

Elephant: Elephant are the world's largest land animal. They are found mostly in dense forests and are always found in groups. The elephants are black in colour with a small tail, two big ears, thick legs, a trunk, and two little eyes. The teeth of the elephants are very costly and are referred to as tusks. According to the report, Karnataka has the highest number of elephants (6,049), followed by Assam (5,719) and Kerala.

Camel: Camel is a domestic animal. Camels have long, curved necks and a high hump. Camels have one or two humps on their back which stores fat. Camels can live up to many days in the desert without food and water. Camels have their body covered with hair and their skin is thick to save them from the severe heat of the desert.It lives in the kutch jungle in gujarat.

Hippopotamus:Hippopotamus is a huge herbivorous animal. It fills its stomach by eating grass, leaves. It has four strong legs, two large bulging eyes, two ears, a nose, and a short tail. It can weigh up to 4 tons. Hippopotamus are strong swimmers. Famous Sanctury in the world for hippo - Kaziranga National Park from assam.

Giraffe:Giraffe is the tallest animal. They are mostly known for their long neck, and an average giraffe can grow up to 5.5 meters in height. Their long neck helps them to keep an eye on predators like lions and hyenas. They have four tall legs. Their favorite foods are leaves. They sleep only 5-30 minutes in one day.

Leopard:Leopard is a carnivorous wild animal. The leopard has four legs, two bright eyes, two ears, and a long tail. Leopard body is dark yellow in colour. Leopards can run at speeds of up to 58 km/h (36 mph).Leopards are very solitary and spend most of their time alone. The leopard is very comfortable climbing trees.

Horse: horse is a swift and strong animal.horse is easily run long distances. A horse is a vegetarian and domestic animal. It is very beneficial as well. A horse has four feet, two eyes, a nose, two ears and a tail. Their feet are quite slim but really strong. It allows them to run fast and for long. If you look at history, you will know the important part they played in it due to their loyalty.

Monkey:Monkeys are intelligent and playful animal.well as very emotional They can jump from one tree to another. Most of the Monkeys live in trees. They can climb a tree quickly, and can easily jump from one tree to another. Monkeys also have a strong brains and they behave like humans. The diet of the monkey includes walnuts, berries, and fruits. Its favorite fruit is bananas.

Tiger:A tiger has a large and strong body. It has a thick yellow coat of fur with dark stripes. Their favorite foods pigs, cows, deer, elephant ,buffalos and goats. It has four legs, a large head, a long striped tail and a pair of glowing, ferocious eyes. Tiger is India's national animal. The “Sundarbans National Park” from Bengal tiger is the most popular species, mainly found in India.

Tiger: A kangaroo is moves by jumping on its back legs. Female kangaroos carry their babies in a pouch on their stomach. They fill their stomach by eating grass, leaves, and fruits, etc. The kangaroo has four legs in which the back two legs are long. The kangaroos walk by jumping on two legs. Kangaroos are mostly found in Australia.

Zebra: Zebra is a wild animal.Zebras fill their stomachs by eating grass, leaves, etc. Their entire body is of black color, on which white stripes are made. Zebra is the most beautiful animal because of their iconic black and white stripes which sets them apart when they are compared to other land mammals.

fox:Fox is a wild animal. It has four legs, two eyes, two straight ears, and a padded tail. The whole body of the fox is full of hair. Its nails and teeth are very sharp, which they use while hunting. It is an omnivorous animal that fills its stomach by eating fruits, vegetables, birds, fish, frogs, insects, etc. Foxes confuse their prey by making different sounds.

Deer: Deer is a very beautiful animal.It lives in the jungle. It has a very beautiful two eyes, two ear, a very small White tail and four legs. Deer is a herbivorous animal that lives in the forest. Deer fill their stomach by eating green leaves, grass, etc. It lives in india= Kanha National Park of Madhya Pradesh, Kaziranga and Manas National Parks of Assam.

Sheep:Sheep is a domestic animal that is reared for business. Sheep is a very useful animal from which we get wool and milk etc. Sheep is a domesticated ruminant mammal with a dense wooly coat and curved horns. It is kept for its wool or meat. . Colors of domestic sheep range from pure white to dark chocolate brown, and even spotted or piebald.

Cat: Cats have very sweet animal. It has two beautiful eyes, adorably tiny paws, sharp claws, and two perky ears which are very sensitive to sounds. It has a tiny body covered with smooth fur and it has a furry tail as well. Cats have an adorable face with a tiny nose, a big mouth and a few whiskers under its nose.
